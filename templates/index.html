Looking for a bite to eat?

Our platform let's you check how busy restaurants in your area are. Avoid long lines and ridiculous waiting times by using our optimized location search algorithm. Save your precious time with BusyBites!

Check restaurant occupation levels
from any location

We all know the feeling of going to a restaurant only to be faced with crowded tables and eternal wait times. No one likes waiting on an empty stomach, so we created BusyBites, a web platform that allows you to check how busy local restaurants are at any location and any time. With our web app, you can plan your meal trips and avoid crowded venues altogether.

How our web tool
works

We process and visualize massive global databases of mobile location information to fetch accurate approximations of restaurant occupancy levels. The best part is we do all of this in real time, meaning you don't have to wait to plan - or wait to eat!
